ONE STEP PRE-ARRANGED KUMITE
Attack Defense
1 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Jodan-Uke - Rising Block, Reverse Punch to Stomach (Kiai).
2 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Reverse Rising Block with your right hand, left leg forward, and right leg back. Grab their wrist, shift into a horse stance while pulling them towards your back leg, and perform a vertical punch to their head (Kiai).
3 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step to your right side and block the punch with a left hand Shuto-Uke. Mae-Geri and Nukite with your front leg and hand (Kiai), and land in Neko-Ashi-Dachi (Cat Stance).
4 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Parry the punch from the outside with your left hand. Step into Kiba-Dachi with the left leg, striking low with your left hand (Kiai), and blocking the punch with a right hand Chudan-Uke.
6th kyu only
5 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Step forward with your right leg, blocking the punch with a high left hand Shuto-Uke, striking with a right Shuto to the neck (Kiai).
