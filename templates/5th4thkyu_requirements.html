ONE STEP PRE-ARRANGED KUMITE
Attack Defense
Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Jodan-Uke - Rising Block, Reverse Punch to Stomach
Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Reverse Rising Block with your right hand, left leg forward, and right leg back. Grab their wrist, shift into a horse stance while pulling them towards your back leg, and perform a vertical punch to their head.
Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step to your right side and block the punch with a left hand Shuto-Uke. Mae-Geri and Nukite with your front leg and hand, and land in Neko-Ashi-Dachi (Cat Stance).
Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Parry the punch from the outside with your left hand. Step into Kiba-Dachi with the left leg, striking low with your left hand, blocking the punch with a right hand Chudan-Uke.
Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Step forward with your right leg, blocking the punch with a high left hand Shuto-Uke, striking with a right Shuto to the neck.
Mae-Geri - Front Snap Kick Step in with your left leg, catching the attacker's shin in an X-block before their kick extends. Pull back slightly and perform an X-Shuto Strike to their neck before their foot lands.
4th kyu only
Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step back and turn with the right leg, parrying with your right hand. Grab their wrist, and strike with a left hand Shuto to their neck.
