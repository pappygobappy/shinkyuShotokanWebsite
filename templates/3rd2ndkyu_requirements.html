ONE STEP PRE-ARRANGED KUMITE
Attack Defense
1 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Jodan-Uke - Rising Block, Reverse Punch to Stomach (Kiai).
2 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Reverse Rising Block with your right hand, left leg forward, and right leg back. Grab their wrist, shift into a horse stance while pulling them towards your back leg, and perform a vertical punch to their head (Kiai).
3 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step to your right side and block the punch with a left hand Shuto-Uke. Mae-Geri and Nukite with your front leg and hand (Kiai), and land in Neko-Ashi-Dachi (Cat Stance).
4 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Parry the punch from the outside with your left hand. Step into Kiba-Dachi with the left leg, striking low with your left hand (Kiai), and blocking the punch with a right hand Chudan-Uke.
5 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Step forward with your right leg, blocking the punch with a high left hand Shuto-Uke, striking with a right Shuto to the neck (Kiai).
6 Mae-Geri - Front Snap Kick Step in with your left leg, catching the attacker's shin in an X-block before their kick extends. Pull back slightly and perform an X-Shuto Strike to their neck before their foot lands (Kiai).
7 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step back and turn with the right leg, parrying with your right hand. Grab their wrist, and strike with a left hand Shuto to their neck (Kiai).
8 Jodan-Oi-Suki - Face Punch Step forward with your left leg, parrying the punch with your left hand, and grabbing behind the neck with your right. Pull them down while thrusting your knee into their stomach (Kiai).
2nd kyu only
9 Chudan-Oi-Suki - Stomach Punch Step forward with your right leg, blocking the punch with a left hand Chudan-Uke and protecting your front with a right hand Gedan-Barai. Chamber your right hand and push forward into an uppercut to your opponent's stomach (Kiai).
JIYU KUMITE
Free-style sparring between all other brown belts.
